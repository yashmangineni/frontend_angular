Resume Writing Essentials

What to Include

  • ✓ Contact Information
  • ✓ Professional Summary
  • ✓ Work Experience
  • ✓ Education
  • ✓ Skills & Certifications
  • ✓ Relevant Projects

What to Avoid

  • ✗ Personal Photos
  • ✗ Irrelevant Work History
  • ✗ Typos and Grammar Errors
  • ✗ Generic Objective Statements
  • ✗ Outdated Email Addresses
  • ✗ More than 2 Pages

Key Sections

  • 📝 Professional Summary (2-3 lines)
  • 💼 Work Experience (chronological)
  • 🎓 Education & Certifications
  • ⚙️ Technical Skills
  • 🏆 Achievements & Awards
  • 🔗 Portfolio/LinkedIn Links
